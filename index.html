Frequently Asked Questions

What is a VPN and why do I need one?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and the internet, protecting your online activities from prying eyes. You need one to enhance privacy, secure your data on public Wi-Fi, bypass geo-restrictions, and prevent ISP tracking.
